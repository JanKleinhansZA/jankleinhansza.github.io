Geo Photo Snap – GPS Camera

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how CloudMobile ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects your information when you use Geo Photo Snap – GPS Camera ("the App"). The App is designed to capture photographs with GPS location data and related metadata for documentation and evidence purposes.

By using Geo Photo Snap – GPS Camera, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Photos You Capture

The App allows you to capture photographs with embedded metadata including GPS location and timestamps.

  • Photos captured using your device camera
  • Timestamp when the photo was taken
  • GPS coordinates (latitude and longitude)
  • Location address (if available)
  • Device information used to capture the image
Important: All photos are stored locally on your device unless you choose to back them up or export them.

1.2 Location Information

The App uses your device location to add GPS information to photos.

  • Latitude and longitude coordinates
  • GPS accuracy information
  • Optional location address using reverse geocoding

Location access is only used when capturing photos and is required to embed location data into the image.

1.3 Device Information

We may collect limited technical information including:

  • Device model and manufacturer
  • Android operating system version
  • App version
  • Device language settings
  • General system information required for app functionality

1.4 Optional Backup Information

If you enable optional cloud backup, the App may upload photos and metadata files to your personal cloud storage.

For example, backups may be stored in your personal account on services such as:

  • Google Drive

These backups are stored in folders within your own cloud account and are controlled by your cloud provider.

1.5 Metadata Files

The App may generate metadata files alongside photos to store additional information such as:

  • Photo evidence ID
  • Timestamp
  • GPS coordinates
  • Location accuracy
  • Device model
  • App version
  • SHA-256 image hash for integrity verification
These files help maintain documentation and authenticity of captured photos.

2. How We Use Your Information

The collected information is used to provide the functionality of the App:

  • Capture photos with GPS location information
  • Add timestamps and location overlays to photos
  • Generate metadata files for documentation
  • Create PDF or case reports from captured photos
  • Allow optional backup of photos to your personal cloud storage
  • Improve application performance and stability

3. How We Store Your Information

3.1 Local Device Storage

Photos captured by the App are stored locally on your device in the folder:

/Pictures/GeoPhotoSnap/

This allows you full control over your files and enables offline access.

3.2 Metadata Storage

Additional metadata may be stored:

  • Inside the image EXIF metadata
  • In JSON metadata files stored alongside photos
  • In the app's local database

3.3 Optional Cloud Backup

If you enable backup, photos and metadata may be uploaded to your cloud storage account such as Google Drive.

CloudMobile does not have access to your personal cloud account outside of the permissions granted by you.

4. Data Security

We implement measures to protect your information:

  • Secure access to device storage
  • Use of standard Android security practices
  • Optional integrity verification using SHA-256 file hashes
  • Protection against unauthorized access

However, no method of digital storage is completely secure.

5. Third-Party Services

The App may integrate with third-party services including:

  • Google Play Services
  • Google Maps (for location and map snapshots)
  • Google Drive (optional cloud backup)

These services are governed by their own privacy policies.

6. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell or share your personal data with third parties.

Your photos and metadata remain under your control unless you choose to export or share them.

You may share exported reports, photos, or ZIP case files with other parties at your discretion.

7. Data Retention

Your data is stored as follows:

  • Photos remain on your device until you delete them
  • Metadata files remain alongside photos
  • Cloud backups remain until you remove them from your cloud account

8. Permissions Used by the App

The App may request the following Android permissions:

  • Camera – to capture photos
  • Location – to embed GPS coordinates in photos
  • Storage / Media access – to save and manage captured images
  • Internet – required for optional backup and map features

Permissions are used strictly for the functionality of the App.

9. Children's Privacy

The App is not specifically directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
