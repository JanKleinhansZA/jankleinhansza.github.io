Geo Photo Snap – GPS Camera

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how CloudMobile ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects your information when you use Geo Photo Snap – GPS Camera ("the App"). The App allows users to capture photographs with embedded GPS coordinates, timestamps, and metadata for documentation purposes.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Photos You Capture

The App allows you to capture photos with embedded metadata including:

  • Photo images captured using your device camera
  • Timestamp of the photo
  • GPS coordinates
  • Location address (if available)
  • Evidence identification number
Important: Photos remain stored on your device unless you choose to export or back them up.

1.2 Location Information

The App uses your device location to embed GPS coordinates in photos.

  • Latitude and longitude
  • Location accuracy
  • Address information via reverse geocoding

1.3 Device Information

  • Device model and manufacturer
  • Android version
  • App version
  • Language settings

1.4 Metadata Files

The App may create JSON metadata files for documentation purposes containing:

  • Photo evidence ID
  • Timestamp
  • GPS coordinates
  • Device information
  • SHA-256 hash used to verify file integrity

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Capture photos with location data
  • Embed timestamps and GPS overlays
  • Create documentation reports
  • Generate PDF reports
  • Allow export or backup of files
  • Improve app performance

3. How We Store Your Information

Local Device Storage

Photos are stored locally on your device in the folder:

/Pictures/GeoPhotoSnap/

Metadata Storage

  • EXIF metadata inside images
  • JSON files stored next to photos
  • Local app database

Optional Cloud Backup

If enabled, files may be backed up to your personal cloud storage such as Google Drive.

4. Data Security

  • Standard Android security practices
  • Controlled file access
  • Optional SHA-256 integrity verification

5. Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party services including:

  • Google Play Services
  • Google Maps
  • Google Drive

Google Privacy Policy

6. Location Data Usage

The App collects location data only when you capture a photo in order to embed GPS coordinates in the image metadata.

  • Embedding GPS coordinates in images
  • Displaying coordinates on photo overlays
  • Generating reports and map snapshots

The App does not track location continuously.

7. Background Location Usage

Geo Photo Snap does not collect location data in the background.

Location access occurs only while the App is actively used to capture photos.

8. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell or share your personal data with third parties.

Your photos remain under your control unless you choose to export or share them.

9. Data Safety

The App does not sell personal data and does not share photos or location data unless you explicitly export files or enable backup services.

10. Evidence and Documentation Disclaimer

The App is designed to assist users in documenting photos with location and timestamp information.

CloudMobile does not guarantee that photos captured with the App will be accepted as legal evidence in court or legal proceedings.

Users are responsible for verifying the authenticity and admissibility of any documentation created using the App.

The App is not directed toward children under 13 years of age.
