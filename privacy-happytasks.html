Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how CloudMobile ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects your information when you use the Happy Tasks mobile application ("the App"). We are committed to helping you stay organised and productive while protecting your personal data.

By using Happy Tasks, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the App.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide a personalised task management experience and to improve app performance.

1.1 Task and Project Data

The App is designed to help you manage tasks and projects. We store the information you manually enter:

  • Task Titles and Descriptions: The names, notes, and details of tasks you create.
  • Due Dates and Reminders: Dates, times, and recurring schedules you assign to tasks.
  • Projects and Labels: Organisational structures and tags you create to group tasks.
  • Priority Levels: Urgency indicators assigned to individual tasks.
  • Completion Records: Dates and times when tasks are marked as complete.
  • Subtasks: Child tasks created within a parent task.

1.2 Device and Technical Information

We automatically collect certain technical information for stability and core functionality:

  • Device type and Operating System (Android version).
  • App version and Package Name.
  • Device identifiers (required for scheduling reminders and exact alarms).
  • IP address (used generally for regional compliance and security).

1.3 Advertising Information

Happy Tasks uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:

  • Advertising ID: A unique, user-resettable ID for advertising, used to provide personalized or non-personalized ads based on your consent.
  • Interaction Data: Information about ad views and clicks.
  • Approximate Location: Non-precise location derived from IP address for regional ad targeting.

You can manage your ad preferences or reset your Advertising ID in your Android system settings.

1.4 Diagnostic and Analytics Data

To improve app stability, we collect anonymized crash reports and usage patterns to understand which features are most helpful to our users.

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 To Provide the Service

  • To display your tasks, projects, and productivity statistics on the Dashboard.
  • To fire reminders at your selected times using exact alarm permissions.
  • To generate your daily progress report notification showing completed tasks, due today, overdue counts, and your completion streak.
  • To power home screen widgets showing your upcoming and overdue tasks.

2.2 To Improve the App

  • To fix bugs and prevent app crashes.
  • To analyse which features are most commonly used in order to optimise the interface.

2.3 Legal Compliance

  • To comply with regional privacy laws such as GDPR (EU) and CCPA (California) via the integrated Google User Messaging Platform (UMP).

3. Data Storage and Security

Local-First Storage: By default, Happy Tasks stores all your task and project data locally on your device in a secure SQLite database. We do not use a central cloud server to store your personal task data; your information stays on your device.

3.1 Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data stored within the App. However, please remember that no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights and Data Control

5.1 Deletion

You have full control over your data. You can delete individual tasks, clear completed tasks, or uninstall the App at any time to permanently remove all App data from your device.

5.2 Advertising Choices

  • You can reset your Advertising ID through your device settings.
  • You may opt out of personalized ads in your Google account settings.

5.3 Regional Rights

Users in the European Economic Area (EEA) and California are prompted with a consent form upon first launch to manage their data sharing preferences for personalized advertising, in compliance with GDPR and CCPA.

6. Children's Privacy

Happy Tasks is intended for general audiences and is not directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us so it can be removed.
