Privacy Policy for HappyTasks

Last updated: March 2, 2026

HappyTasks ("the App") is a task management application designed to help users organize tasks and improve productivity. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when you use the app.

1. Information We Collect

a) Personal Information

HappyTasks does not require account registration and does not directly collect personal information such as your name, address, or phone number.

b) App Data

Tasks and related information you create are stored locally on your device unless you choose to use external backup services provided by your device or operating system.

c) Advertising and Analytics Data

The app uses third-party advertising services which may collect certain information automatically, including:

  • Device type and operating system
  • Advertising identifiers
  • App interactions and usage statistics
  • Approximate location (non-precise)

2. Advertising

HappyTasks uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may use cookies, advertising IDs, and similar technologies to provide personalized or non-personalized ads.

Learn more about how Google uses data here:
https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

3. How We Use Information

Information collected may be used to:

  • Provide and maintain app functionality
  • Improve app performance and stability
  • Display advertisements
  • Detect crashes and technical issues

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. Data may be shared only with trusted third-party services necessary for app operation, such as:

  • Google AdMob (advertising services)
  • Google Play Services

5. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect information from unauthorized access or disclosure. However, no electronic transmission or storage method is completely secure.

6. Children's Privacy

HappyTasks is not specifically directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so it can be removed.

7. Your Choices

  • You can reset your advertising ID through your device settings.
  • You may opt out of personalized ads in your Google account settings.
  • You may uninstall the app at any time to stop data collection.

8. Third-Party Links

The app may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party sites.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
