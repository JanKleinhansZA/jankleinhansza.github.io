Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how CloudMobile ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects your information when you use Budget Application ("the App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

By using Budget Application, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the App.

1. Information We Collect

We collect several types of information to provide and improve our services:

1.1 Account Information

When you create an account or sign in to Budget Application, we collect:

  • Email address - Used for account creation, login, password recovery, and important notifications
  • Password - Securely hashed and stored (we never store passwords in plain text)
  • Display name - If you choose to provide it
  • Profile photo - If you upload one or use one from Google Sign-In

1.2 Google Sign-In Information

If you choose to sign in with Google, we receive:

  • Your Google account email address
  • Your name and profile picture (if you grant permission)
  • Google user ID (for authentication purposes)

We use this information solely for authentication and account creation. We do not access your Google contacts, emails, or other Google services.

1.3 Financial Data You Enter

To provide budgeting and expense tracking features, we store the financial information you manually enter into the App:

  • Budget categories and amounts
  • Income and expense transactions
  • Account names and balances (e.g., "Checking Account," "Savings")
  • Payment methods and categories
  • Financial goals and targets
  • Notes and descriptions you add to transactions
  • Recurring transactions and bills
  • Custom categories and tags
Important: We do NOT collect, store, or have access to your actual bank account credentials, credit card numbers, banking passwords, or any sensitive financial authentication information. All financial data is manually entered by you.

1.4 Device and Usage Information

We automatically collect certain technical information:

  • Device type, model, and manufacturer
  • Operating system version (Android version)
  • App version you're using
  • Device identifiers (such as Android ID or Firebase Instance ID)
  • Screen resolution and device language settings
  • IP address (for security and regional features)
  • App usage statistics (features used, screens viewed, session duration)
  • Time zone and general location (country/region, not precise GPS)

1.5 Subscription and Payment Information

When you purchase a subscription, we collect:

  • Subscription type (monthly, yearly, or lifetime)
  • Purchase date and renewal dates
  • Transaction ID from Google Play Store
  • Subscription status (active, expired, cancelled)

Note: Payment processing is handled entirely by Google Play Store. We do not collect or store your credit card information, billing address, or payment credentials.

1.6 Advertising Information (Free Users Only)

If you use the free version of Budget Application with ads, Google AdMob may collect:

  • Advertising ID (for personalized ads)
  • Device information and IP address
  • Ad interaction data (views, clicks)
  • General location (for regional ad targeting)

Premium subscribers do not see ads and this data is not collected for them.

1.7 Crash Reports and Diagnostics

To improve app stability, we collect crash and error reports through Firebase Crashlytics:

  • Crash logs and stack traces
  • Device state at the time of crash
  • App version and device information
  • Time and frequency of crashes

This information is anonymized and used solely for debugging and improving app performance.

1.8 Analytics Data

We use Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with the App:

  • Which features are most used
  • User flow through the app
  • Time spent in different sections
  • App performance metrics

This data is collected in aggregate form and helps us improve the user experience.

3. How We Store Your Information

3.1 Local Storage

Your financial data is stored locally on your device in an encrypted SQLite database. This allows you to:

  • Access your budgets offline
  • Enjoy fast app performance
  • Maintain full control over your data

Local data remains on your device until you delete the app or clear app data.

3.2 Cloud Storage (Firebase)

For registered users, we also store data in Firebase Cloud Firestore to enable:

  • Automatic backup of your financial data
  • Synchronization across multiple devices
  • Account recovery if you lose your device
  • Seamless switching between devices

Cloud data is stored on secure Google Firebase servers, which may be located in various countries worldwide.

3.3 Data Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encryption in transit: All data transmitted between your device and our servers uses SSL/TLS encryption
  • Encryption at rest: Data stored in Firebase is encrypted
  • Secure authentication: Passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms
  • Access controls: Strict access controls limit who can access user data
  • Regular security audits: We regularly review and update security measures
Important: While we implement strong security measures, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, and you use the App at your own risk.
