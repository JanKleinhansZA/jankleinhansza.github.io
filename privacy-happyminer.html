Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how CloudMobile ("we," "us," or "our") handles your information when you use the Happy Miner mobile application ("the App"). Happy Miner is an idle mining game. We are committed to being transparent about our data practices.

By using Happy Miner, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the App.

Simple Summary: Happy Miner does not collect any personal information from you. Your game progress is stored locally on your device. The only data collection that occurs is by Google AdMob for the purpose of displaying advertisements.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Game Data

Happy Miner stores your game progress — such as your crystal count, mine shaft levels, world progression, and prestige data — locally on your device. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to CloudMobile or any server we operate.

1.2 Advertising Information

Happy Miner uses Google AdMob to display banner advertisements and rewarded video ads. AdMob may collect the following on our behalf:

  • Advertising ID: A unique, user-resettable identifier used to deliver and personalise advertisements.
  • Ad Interaction Data: Information about ad impressions, views, and clicks.
  • Device and Network Information: Such as device type, operating system version, and general location, used by AdMob for ad targeting and fraud prevention.

You can reset or opt out of personalised advertising at any time via your Android device settings under Privacy > Ads.

1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

CloudMobile does not collect, store, or process any of the following:

  • Your name, email address, or any other personal identifiers.
  • Your location data.
  • Your contacts, camera, or microphone.
  • Any data entered outside of the game itself.

2. How We Use Information

CloudMobile uses your locally stored game data solely to provide the core game experience — saving and restoring your progress between sessions. We do not use this data for analytics, marketing, or any other purpose.

Advertising data collected by Google AdMob is used by Google to serve relevant advertisements within the App. CloudMobile receives aggregate, anonymised reporting (e.g. total ad impressions) but never receives any personally identifiable information from AdMob.

3. Ads Grace Period

To provide a positive first experience, Happy Miner withholds banner advertisements for a short period after a new install. During this grace period, a first-launch timestamp is stored locally on your device in the App's private SharedPreferences storage. This timestamp is never transmitted off your device and is used only to determine when the grace period has expired.

4. Data Storage and Security

Local-Only Storage: All game data is stored exclusively on your device in the App's private local storage. CloudMobile does not operate any servers that receive or store your game data.

Because your data never leaves your device, the primary security measure is your device's own security (screen lock, encryption). Uninstalling the App will permanently delete all locally stored game data.

5. Third-Party Services

The only third-party service integrated into Happy Miner is Google AdMob. We do not use any analytics SDKs, crash reporting services, or social login providers.

6. GDPR and CCPA Compliance

Happy Miner integrates the Google User Messaging Platform (UMP) to present a consent form to users in the European Economic Area (EEA) and California before any personalised advertising is served. You may withdraw or change your consent at any time by clearing the App's data via your device settings.

Since CloudMobile itself does not collect personal data, your rights under GDPR (access, rectification, erasure, portability) as they relate to CloudMobile are fully satisfied by the fact that no such data exists on our systems. For data held by Google AdMob, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy linked above.

7. Children's Privacy

Happy Miner is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. As CloudMobile does not collect any personal data, no such collection from children can occur through our systems. AdMob is configured to serve non-personalised ads only in contexts where child-directed treatment may apply.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
